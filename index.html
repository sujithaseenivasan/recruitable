Offerings

Unlike most career prep services that are only focused on interview prep and theoretical content, Recruitable offers:

  • 360-degree curriculum covering all aspects of desired roles (Product or SWE).
  • Personalized career strategy for every student.
  • Multiple projects to gain real experience and fill in resume gaps.
  • Resume review until it’s perfect.
  • Intensive interview preparation and mocks with experts.
  • Up-to-date recruiting strategies specific to your dream company.
  • Continued support and feedback after program completion.
  • Eventual alumni network across top tech companies for placement.

About Recruitable

Recruitable helps college students land their dream internships in tech through an intensive 2 month program taught by vetted student instructors that have already secured internships at top tech companies. As students ourselves, we deeply understand our customers and will work with them until they feel fully ready. Their journey matters to us, and we genuinely want them to succeed.
